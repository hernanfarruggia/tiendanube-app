## Notes

### Cloudflare Tunnel Setup
- **Key Decision**: Used custom domain (wearekadre.com) instead of cfargotunnel.com URLs
- **Why**: Tiendanube CSP policy blocks cfargotunnel.com in iframes, only allows *.cloudflare.com and specific whitelisted domains
- **Alternative Considered**: LocalTunnel - rejected in favor of Cloudflare for stability
- Tunnel persists across restarts, DNS routes are permanent
